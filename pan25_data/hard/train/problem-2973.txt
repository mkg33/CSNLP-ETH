Ukraine’s successful counteroffensive against Russian forces continues to surprise and impress.
It surprises for the military’s ability to emerge from six years of grinding war as a force that’s agile, strategic and lethal.
And it has impressed with how it’s been able counter Russian President Vladimir Putin’s propaganda machine, Russia’s brutal attacks on civilians, and has rallied the United States and Western allies to arm, train and plan.
