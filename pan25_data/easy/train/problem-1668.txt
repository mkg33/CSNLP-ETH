Japan has continuously reevaluated what Article 9 really means.

Somewhat recently they have also begun expanding their “navy” under the guise of coast guard and rescue vessels that while not war capable, could become so very quickly, most notably helicopter carriers that are most definitely not aircraft carriers and could not be converted to ones.
