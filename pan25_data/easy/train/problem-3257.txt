It’s less about ‘tells’ and more about your primal response to them.. the first impressions you get of their body language, their micro-expressions and word choices are factors that your subconscious will pick up and send signals to your brain, typically very accurate.
If you’re in a situation where you find yourself talking yourself down from alarm or trying to justify or excuse someone’s behavior, even if it’s something you don’t pick up right away, listen to the instincts telling you to leave/run/have your guard up.
it will serve you well.
There’s a great book about this very topic called ‘The Gift of Fear’ the author goes into detail about these small seemingly insignificant triggers.
