The people affected by the train derailment in East Palestine, Ohio, do not qualify for direct financial aid from the Federal Emergency Management Agency.
But FEMA and other federal agencies have been assisting since the accident there in early February.
Social media posts have misleadingly claimed that the federal government has denied aid.
>Today’s announcement builds on the federal government’s ongoing response efforts to the East Palestine train derailment.
At President Biden’s direction, interagency teams have been out in the community of East Palestine ensuring residents receive up-to-date information.
Over the past few days, teams have interacted with more than 530 households, distributing flyers, answering questions, and directing residents to available resources.
